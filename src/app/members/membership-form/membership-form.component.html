Who? How?

To become a member of l'Union Alsacienne of New-York you must be:

  • born in Alsace
  • of Alsatian descent
  • married to an Alsatian

And pay a dues of $40/year.

By becoming a member, you're invited to our events with a special price and subscribe to our quarterly bulletin.
